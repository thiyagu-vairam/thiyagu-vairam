AgriPix: AI-Powered Design Innovation

An AI Image Generation Tool Saving $2M Annually for UPL’s Marketing Team

About the Project

AgriPix is transforming visual content creation at UPL by replacing stock images and agency reliance with AI-generated, on-brand visuals in minutes. Designed to cut ~$2M in annual design costs, this generative AI tool empowers marketers to create high-quality assets, seasonal creatives, and promos quickly—boosting savings, adoption, and creative independence across teams.

My Role

As the sole Product Designer for AgriPix, I led the end-to-end design—from ideation to iterative prototyping—crafting a seamless prompt-based image generation experience. I collaborated closely with stakeholders to align the tool with business needs, optimized workflows, and ensured a smooth rollout. I also created tutorial videos to onboard internal teams effectively.

Business Objective

Optimize Marketing Spend

Lower creative production costs by reducing dependence on external agencies

Enhance Efficiency

Empower teams to deliver more with less—faster, leaner marketing execution.

Improve Speed-to-Market

Accelerate campaign launches with AI-generated visuals on demand.

Maximize Campaign Performance

Enable rapid creative testing to identify and scale top-performing assets.

User Challenges

Our users are UPL’s internal Marketing Managers, Business/Sales Leads

Slow Turnaround Times

Agencies juggle multiple clients, causing delays and missed opportunities.

Endless Back-and-Forth

Quality output often demands multiple iterations and meetings.

Lack of Creative Control

Marketers often rely on external teams, limiting agility and quick experimentation

Missed Seasonal Deadlines

Rigid timelines make it hard to capitalize on time-sensitive campaigns.

Why Generative AI?

Customization & Uniqueness

The AI model reflects UPL’s brand identity for consistent visuals.

Cost Efficiency

Eliminates recurring licensing fees of stock images.

Empowers Non-Design Teams

Allows different teams to create professional visuals.

Scalability & Speed

Generates multiple variations quickly without time-consuming.

Flexibility & Iteration

Allows fine-tuning and style variations based on feedback.

Avoids Copyright issues

Produces original content, reducing legal risks.

Identified Use Cases

Use Case Description Tech used
Prompt to Image User want to generate creative based on prompt Uses Mid-Journey, Dall-E, Claid.ai for images
Image to Prompt User shares reference image to generate creatives Uses ChatGPT which converts image to text
Resize / Enhance Upscale and Enhance the image appearance Uses Vance.ai to enhance the images
Customize Image User wants to edit the image Canva editor to finalise & download
Image options Orientation, Style of the output Internal model & Mid-Journey / Dall-E / Claid.ai
Access history Accessing the earlier prompts / generated images
Save as favourites Save images for future use
Support Global Language User wants to prompt on regional language
