Prime Membership for Retailers

Driving GMV and User Engagement with nurture.retail Prime Membership

About the Project

nurture.retail needed a way to increase the user engagement and retention while providing additional value to loyal customers. The goal was to create a membership model that would offer tangible benefits to the users and differentiate it from competitors.

My Role

Led end-to-end design — from strategy and user journey planning to execution and visual design — delivering a seamless Prime Membership experience.

Business Objective

GMV & ARPU

Boosting revenue and maximizing value for every retailer.

Retain Users

Building trust and consistency to keep users coming back.

User Engagement

Creating experiences that keep users active and inspired

Build customer loyalty

Fostering long-term relationships to increase lifetime value.

Strategic Takeaways

Early Stakeholder Alignment

Frequent check-ins with business teams helped clarify revenue expectations, define KPIs, and align priorities before moving into execution.

Two-Stage Validation Approach

First, we tested concept demand through a homepage campaign, then introduced the membership as a product to assess real purchase intent.

Milestone-Based Rollout Strategy

We broke the program into smaller phases, allowing us to build, test, and adapt quickly—making adoption smoother and more effective.

Design-Driven Communication

UI elements like banners, tooltips, and progress meters were used to nudge users, improve clarity, and boost participation and renewals.
